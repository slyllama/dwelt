# Dwelt: Iron Solstice

Cleanse and rebuild a vibrant but fractured world in *Dwelt: Iron Solstice*, a steampunk puzzle-solving and decorating game. Take your place amongst a band of armatures - robots who have determined to heal and rejuvenate an overgrown earth for the eventual return of evacuated activists and scientists - and defend it against those who would seek to reignite war.

 - **Explore a broken yet healing world,** and build connections with the space-bound itinerants who yearn to re-establish themselves there. Aid in their efforts to rehabilitate the land by clearing key zones, designing, and building to their specifications.
 - **Cleanse and build in a living environment where everything dynamic.** Buildings, machines, creatures, and natural features all share the same language of conditions and effects, making the process of clearing and building an engaging, open-ended puzzle-solving effort.
 - **Unlock building pieces and decorations through cleansing the world.** Use them to decorate your own hub world with powerful building tools like colour palettes, decoration variations, and more. Take on optional challenges to acquire special trophies and ornaments.
